Family A — Native Liquid Glass: segmented & switch

Take the idioms a Mac user already trusts — the segmented control and the switch — and render them in larger, beautiful Liquid Glass, so the Sunset / Always-on choice feels native to macOS Tahoe Settings, just elevated. Restrained → bold.
