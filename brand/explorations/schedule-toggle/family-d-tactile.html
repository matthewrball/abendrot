Family D — Tactile glass mechanisms

The bold, expressive end — still Liquid Glass. One satisfying, physical-feeling control per concept: a chunky switch, dial, rocker, slider, or sun-on-a-horizon that snaps between Sunset and Always on with springy, tactile motion and an ember glow — distinctive, but rendered in glass so it still feels Apple.

D1 · Glass sun switch
Schedule
When Abendrot warms your displays.

Sunset warms automatically around your local sunset — easing in beforehand and holding through the night — using your time zone to estimate sunrise and sunset. No location permission required. Always on keeps warmth on around the clock.

One-line rationale: the most literal idea — flip a big glass switch and the sun glides from dusk to a steady blaze, the whole track warming behind glass.
