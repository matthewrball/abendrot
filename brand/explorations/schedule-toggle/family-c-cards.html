Family C — Choice cards

Present the either-or as two large, premium side-by-side Liquid-Glass tiles you deliberately pick between — the way macOS Settings shows the Light / Dark appearance tiles. Bigger, more legible and more artful than a pill, and unmistakably native: the chosen card lights up with the warm sunset fill, a specular sheen, an ember glow and a drawn-in check; the other stays clear glass.
