Finalist D2 — Rotary ember dial

A tactile Liquid-Glass knob for the Sunset ↔ Always-on choice. Two labelled detents at the base, an ember arc that fills between them, a certain spring-detent snap on landing, and a strong readout + segmented shortcuts beneath so a binary choice is unmistakable. Lead version first, then three micro-variations exploring the arc, the knob face, and the readout.
